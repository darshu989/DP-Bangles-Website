About DP Bangles

Welcome to DP Bangles – where tradition meets craftsmanship.

At DP Bangles, we celebrate the timeless beauty of glass bangles, handcrafted with love and precision. Rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Firozabad, our brand is dedicated to reviving and promoting India’s traditional bangle-making artistry. Each piece we create is not just an accessory, but a reflection of elegance, emotion, and expression.

Our journey began with a simple vision — to bring premium quality glass bangles to every woman who values grace and tradition. With a focus on ethical craftsmanship, we work closely with skilled artisans to ensure every bangle is durable, comfortable, and vibrant. From classic designs to contemporary patterns, our collections are curated to suit every occasion, from daily wear to festive celebrations.

DP Bangles isn’t just about jewelry — it’s about empowering identity and celebrating femininity. As we continue to grow, our mission remains clear: to blend traditional art with modern aesthetics while offering exceptional service. Whether you’re shopping for yourself or gifting someone special, DP Bangles promises unmatched beauty, value, and a connection to India’s cultural soul.

How to Measure

  • Using a Bangle: Measure the inner diameter of a well-fitting bangle.
  • Using Your Hand: Touch your thumb to your pinky, measure around the widest part of your hand, and match it to the chart.
